Residential Program

Help Families Save on Their Electric Bills

Our residential program connects homeowners with community solar - giving them guaranteed savings with zero installation, zero upfront costs, and zero hassle.

How It Works

Community solar allows homeowners to benefit from solar energy without installing panels on their roof. They subscribe to a local solar farm and receive credits on their electric bill - typically saving 10-15% every month.

As an Aminaura rep, you help families discover this program and sign up. It is a genuine win-win: they save money, you earn commission, and clean energy expands.
