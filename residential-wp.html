How It Works

Community solar lets homeowners benefit from solar energy without installing panels. They subscribe to a local solar farm and receive credits on their electric bill - typically saving 10-15% monthly.

As an Aminaura rep, you help families discover this program and sign up. Win-win: they save money, you earn commission, clean energy expands.
